In 2025, I’m aiming to turn my passion for coding into a full-fledged career as a developer. Step by step, I’m building the skills and mindset needed to achieve my dream of becoming a successful developer.

Why Web Development?

I chose web development because I love creating things that people can actually use and interact with online. It’s exciting to see my code come to life in the browser, and I enjoy the mix of creativity and logic it offers.

I will Do Coding and Change The World

I believe coding gives me the power to solve real-world problems and make a meaningful impact. Through technology, I want to build solutions that help people and contribute to a better future.

My Inspiration

My inspiration comes from seeing how technology transforms lives and connects the world in powerful ways. I’m motivated by my friend who started from scratch and built amazing things—I want to follow that path too.

My Current Target

Right now, my goal is to master the fundamentals of web development, especially HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I’m focused on building real projects, improving every day, and preparing myself for a strong start in my developer journey.
